Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Review Replier ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Review text you submit: Processed to generate responses. Not permanently stored.
  • Usage data: Anonymous statistics to improve our service.
  • Email address: If you sign up for a paid plan.
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe. We never store card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI-powered review responses
  • To manage your subscription and account
  • To send product updates and service notices
  • To improve and develop our service
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. AI Processing

Review text is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI API to generate responses, subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. We recommend not including sensitive personal information in reviews you submit.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share only with: Anthropic (AI processing), Stripe (payments), and hosting/analytics providers bound by confidentiality.

5. Data Retention

Review text is not permanently stored. Account information is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

6. Cookies

We use only essential cookies to operate the service. No tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Your Rights

You may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data. Contact us at privacy@reviewreplier.com to exercise these rights.

8. Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS. We implement industry-standard security measures.

9. Children's Privacy

Review Replier is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes

We may update this policy and will notify you of significant changes by email or site notice.
